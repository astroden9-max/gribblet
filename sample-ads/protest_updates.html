GROUND SITREP & LOGS

Bypassing censored broadcast networks. Real-time situation logs from Jantar Mantar, Parliament March routes, and regional university campuses.

HOSPITAL ENGAGEMENT JULY 22, 2026

Wangchuk Sets Fast Conditions; Metro Shutdowns & Detention

Transferred to Medanta Hospital under HC order, Sonam Wangchuk agreed to break his 25-day fast only upon written guarantees that no retaliatory legal cases will be filed against youth protesters. Union Ministers visited him, while 16 Metro stations remained shut and opposition leaders were detained outside the PM Residence.

JUDICIAL HEARING JULY 22, 2026

Delhi High Court Issues Notice Over Police Crackdown

The Delhi High Court issued formal notices to the Centre and Delhi Police on PILs alleging brutal force, unbadged personnel, and lathi-charges on July 20. The court strictly mandated the immediate preservation of all CCTV and videography records from central Delhi.

CRACKDOWN INCIDENT JULY 21, 2026

Police Attack Vehicle Carrying Dipke, Shabana Azmi, & Prakash Raj

Video documentation showed police targeting a truck carrying CJP leaders and public figures, violently dragging and beating non-violent supporters. Founder Abhijeet Dipke issued a public apology to injured youth, while CJP condemned unproductive 5-hour talks with Minister J.P. Nadda.

JANTAR MANTAR JULY 21, 2026

Protesters Rebuild Stage & Mobilize Sit-In

Following police sweeps that tore down temporary shelters, CJP activists and student unions reclaimed Jantar Mantar overnight, rebuilding the main stage to resume sit-ins, hunger strikes, and solidarity rallies.

SANSAD CHALO JULY 20, 2026

100,000 Students March; Police Deploy Blackouts & Severe Force

Over 100,000 students attempted to march to Parliament demanding Dharmendra Pradhan's resignation over NEET leaks. Police responded with welded barricades, internet jammers, tear gas, and indiscriminate lathi-charges by unbadged personnel, leaving dozens hospitalized at RML and Lady Hardinge.

MINISTERIAL TALKS JULY 20, 2026

CJP Delegation Submits Formal Charter to J.P. Nadda

CJP spokespersons met with Union Health Minister J.P. Nadda to present demands: complete overhaul of national exam systems, immediate release of detained hunger strikers, compensation for families of paper leak victims, and ministerial resignation.

HUNGER STRIKE JUNE 28, 2026

Sonam Wangchuk & Student Leaders Begin Fast

Activist Sonam Wangchuk joined CJP and student bodies (AISA, SFI) in an indefinite hunger strike at Jantar Mantar, sparking student solidarity actions and rallies across multiple states.

CENSORSHIP RESPONSE JUNE 21, 2026

CJP Launches Flash Demonstrations Over X Account Ban

After authorities ordered the withholding of CJP's official X account in India at nearly 100,000 followers, student cells coordinated campus flash protests across JNU, DU, BHU, and UoH demanding digital freedom.

STREET MOVEMENT JUNE 6, 2026

CJP Transitions to Street Protests with Satirical Rallies

Transitioning from social media to ground agitation, volunteers launched costume marches and urban clean-up drives, bringing national attention to NEET-UG paper leaks and systemic youth unemployment.

FOUNDATION MAY 16, 2026

Movement Founded as Satirical "Cockroach Janta Party"

Created by digital strategist Abhijeet Dipke in response to judicial remarks labeling protesting youth as "cockroaches and parasites," CJP launched online under "Voice of the Lazy & Unemployed," gaining millions of followers within days.
